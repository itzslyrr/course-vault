AI summary · headless Claude the pulse

Agents are moving from demos to daily tools — the hard part is now reliability.

People are shipping agents into real workflows (support triage, coding, research), but the loudest debate is evaluation and trust: how do you know an autonomous loop did the right thing? Multi-agent setups and tool-use are the momentum; cost is falling fast.
