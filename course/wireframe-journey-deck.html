The idea

Design one screen first

Not the whole app — just one honest view: "recent notes." Open it, see what's new at a glance, click to read. Get one screen right, the rest follows.

one view · one job
🎤 Say: "I didn't try to design everything. I picked the single most useful screen — recent notes — and focused only on that."

The core idea

A wireframe = a floor plan

A rough sketch of a screen — grey boxes and labels only. No colour, no real words, no code. Like an architect's floor plan before the house exists. It answers one question: "what goes where?"

cheap to draw · easy to throw away · settles the layout before you build
🎤 Say: "A wireframe looks unfinished on purpose. It's just boxes showing where things sit — so I can decide the layout in minutes instead of rebuilding real code."
