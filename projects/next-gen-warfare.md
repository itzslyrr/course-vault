# Next-Gen Warfare

**One-liner:** a non-sensitive, public-data missile knowledge base — 11 systems profiled across inventory/classes/armory, with photos, custom SVG infographics, and an 11-slide presentation — built in one day by instructing AI instead of working manually in the terminal.

## Current status
As of 2026-07-02: complete and live at **github.com/itzslyrr/Next-gen-warfare** (public, 4 commits). Presentation upcoming — the deck doubles as the story of *how* it was built.
