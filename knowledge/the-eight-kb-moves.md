# The Eight Knowledge-Base Moves

A method for building knowledge bases that are **readable by humans and consumable by AI**:

1. **Vault** — one plain-text home, under git. Portable, diffable, greppable.
2. **Frontmatter** — a few YAML labels per file (`type`, `tags`, dates, domain fields) the AI filters on.
3. **Wikilink** `[[ ]]` — connections between notes the AI can walk.
4. **Index** — a front-door hub file that every new note gets wired into.
5. **Template** — structure by default; new files never start blank.
6. **Skill** — a capability (pipeline) the AI runs itself, consistently.
7. **CLAUDE.md** — standing rules that travel with the folder.
8. **The doorway** — a human reviews every AI write before it lands.

## Why it matters
The moves turn a pile of files into a system: the AI can find, filter, extend, and maintain it without re-reading everything, and the human keeps final control. Both this vault and [[next-gen-warfare]] are built on them.
