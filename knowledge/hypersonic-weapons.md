# Hypersonic Weapons

"Hypersonic" ≠ just fast. Every ICBM warhead re-enters at Mach 20+. A true hypersonic weapon sustains **Mach 5+ *and maneuvers* inside the atmosphere** — the combination that defeats defenses, because interceptors rely on predicting flight paths.

Two real types:
- **HGV (boost-glide):** rocket lofts a wedge-shaped glider that skips and steers through the upper atmosphere. Examples: Russia's Avangard, China's DF-17, USA's Dark Eagle.
- **HCM (hypersonic cruise):** scramjet engine sustains Mach 5+ the whole flight. Example: Russia's Zircon.
- *(Marketing type: quasi-ballistic missiles like Kinzhal get called hypersonic; Ukraine's Patriots have intercepted them.)*

They exploit the **altitude gap** (~20–60 km): above air defense, below ballistic-missile-defense radar geometry.

## Why it matters
It's the defining strategic weapons race of the 2020s, and a good example of how a category label ("hypersonic") can hide the real distinction (maneuverability, not speed).
