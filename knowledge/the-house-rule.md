# The House Rule

**Reads run free, writes wait for your review.** When an AI tool acts on your vault, any verb that *reads* (a lookup, a recall) can run on its own — nothing changes, so there is nothing to check. Any verb that *writes* (a new note, a decision record) must **stop after drafting** and hand the change back to you: you read the `git diff` and you commit it. The AI drafts; you sign.

## Why it matters
It keeps the human as the final gate (the eighth of [[the-eight-kb-moves]] — "the doorway") while still letting the AI move fast on everything safe. Today that STOP is only a promise the tool keeps because its instructions say so; a guard hook can later make the stop mandatory instead of optional.
